MINABAZAR FAQ

Q: What are the delivery options available?
A: We offer standard and express delivery options. Choose at checkout.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

A: You can return items within 30 days of receiving them.

A: Please contact our customer service with your order details to initiate a return.

A: Refer to our sizing chart available on each product page.

Q: What if I need a different size?
A: You can exchange your item for a different size within the return period.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept credit cards, PayPal, and direct bank transfers.

Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Yes, we use SSL encryption to protect your payment information.
